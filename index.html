About Me

Hi, my name is Adrian Halgas, and I’m currently pursuing my M.S. in Computer Science at Northeastern University, with a background in Electrical Engineering from Cleveland State University. My academic and professional journey has given me a strong foundation in both software development and hardware systems, allowing me to work at the intersection of engineering, automation, and problem-solving.

What excites me most about software development is its power to create efficient, scalable solutions that can optimize complex systems and improve everyday life. Whether it’s automating industrial processes, enhancing robotics, or designing software to integrate hardware seamlessly, I love the challenge of breaking down problems and developing innovative, reliable solutions. I’m particularly drawn to roles that involve software-hardware integration, automation, and robotics, where I can leverage my technical expertise to build high-performance systems. I thrive in collaborative, fast-paced environments, and I’m always eager to learn new technologies that push the boundaries of efficiency and automation.
